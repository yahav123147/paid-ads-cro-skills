## What to study in this corpus

The common denominator across every ad here is the **first 3 lines**.

Every entry opens with a "crazy hook" that stops the scroll — and each one
uses a different angle to do it. The hook ANGLES vary (pain, aspiration, fear,
curiosity, social proof, paradox, recursive proof, stat shock, pattern interrupt).
The hook FORCE never does.

Read each entry and ask:
1. What do the first 3 lines do to me? (Surprise? Curiosity? Recognition?)
2. Why couldn't I stop reading after line 1?
3. What's the angle — and would it work in MY niche?

This corpus is not for copying text. It is for internalizing the 3-line hook
discipline — and the variety of angles that can power it.
