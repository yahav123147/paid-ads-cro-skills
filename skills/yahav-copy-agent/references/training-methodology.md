# Copywriting Methodology — Complete Reference

## Iron Rule #1: Clarity > Creativity
If you must choose between a clear sentence and a creative one — always choose clear.
The reader must understand in 3 seconds: what the product does, what changes for them, and why now.

---

## 3-Step Pre-Writing Workflow

### Step 1: Audience Analysis (MANDATORY)
Before writing a single word, answer these 6 questions:

1. **What keeps them up at night?** — The specific problem, not the category
2. **What secret dreams do they have?** — What they want but are embarrassed to say
3. **What are their deep fears?** — About the problem, about trying again
4. **What do they REALLY want?** — Beyond their declared need (status? freedom? control?)
5. **What do they try repeatedly and fail?** — Past solutions that didn't work
6. **What would make them feel understood?** — The sentence that makes them say "he's talking about ME"

### Step 2: Mine Diamonds from Data
Read the client's answers 3 times. Search for 6 types of diamonds:

| Diamond | Signals | Effect | Example |
|---------|---------|--------|---------|
| **Exclusivity** | "first time", "only time", "never taught this" | FOMO + urgency | "וזאת הפעם הראשונה והיחידה שאני הולך ללמד את זה בלייב" |
| **Authority** | Years, revenue, clients, brands | Instant trust | "משפכי שיווק שהכניסו מעל 100,000,000 ש״ח" |
| **Innovation** | New method, paradigm shift, first in market | Curiosity + leader positioning | "VIBE CODING — קטגוריה שלא הייתה קיימת" |
| **Scarcity** | Limited spots, deadline, closing soon | Loss aversion | "יש רק 15 מקומות בכל מחזור" |
| **Proof** | Screenshots, results, testimonials, before/after | Turns claims into facts | "פי 25 במחזור החודשי — תוך 4 שבועות" |
| **Contrast** | Old way vs new, competitor price vs yours | Clarifies value | "מעצב ₪3,500 + קופירייטר ₪4,000 = ₪9,000. כאן: ₪1,297" |

**Rule:** If you find a diamond — DON'T bury it in a paragraph. Elevate it to a headline, a standalone line, a bold statement. Clients often bury gold in throwaway sentences.

### Step 3: Choose Unique Angle
Every page needs a DIFFERENT creative wrapper. Same marketing message, completely different wrapping each time.

#### 10 Proven Angles

**1. הודעת פטירה / הספד (Death Announcement)**
- Kills the old method in formal-sarcastic format
- Opening: "בצער רב ויגון קודר, אני מודיע על פטירת עיצוב האלמנטור..."
- Works because: Pattern interrupt + humor + loss aversion + escalation
- Best for: Product that REPLACES an old tool/method

**2. וידוי / חשיפה אישית (Confession)**
- Presenter reveals something shocking about the industry
- Opening: "אני הולך להגיד משהו שיזעזע הרבה אנשים בתעשייה..."
- Works because: Curiosity + authenticity + sense of privacy
- Best for: Industry insider with contrarian viewpoint

**3. מכתב אישי (Personal Letter)**
- First person, like a message to a friend
- Opening: "תקשיב, אני צריך לספר לך משהו..."
- Works because: Intimacy + breaks the wall between writer and reader
- Best for: Personal brands, warm audience

**4. מודעת דרושים הפוכה (Reverse Job Ad)**
- Job listing format — the reader mentally "applies"
- Opening: "דרוש: בעל עסק שעייף לשלם אלפים למעצבים..."
- Works because: Familiar format in unexpected context
- Best for: B2B, business audience

**5. כתב אישום / משפט (Indictment)**
- Legal format with "evidence" and "testimony"
- Opening: "כתב אישום נגד: השיטה הישנה לבניית פאנלים..."
- Works because: Drama + clear structure + reader is the judge
- Best for: Products with a strong "enemy" to put on trial

**6. דו"ח חקירה (Investigation Report)**
- Journalistic format with data and sources
- Opening: "חקירה: מה באמת עולה לך הפאנל הבא שלך?"
- Works because: Credibility + curiosity + truth-revealing feel
- Best for: Data-driven products

**7. מכתב פרידה (Breakup Letter)**
- Emotional-funny letter to old tool/service/person
- Opening: "מכתב פרידה מהמעצבת שלי..."
- Works because: Storytelling + identification + humor
- Best for: Switching from one service to another

**8. שידור חדשות (News Broadcast)**
- News format presenting new reality as fact
- Opening: "חדשות 2026: 73% מהפאנלים נבנים ללא מעצב..."
- Works because: Authority + urgency + FOMO
- Best for: Industry disruption, trend-based products

**9. "מה אם..." (What If...)**
- Scenario that sounds impossible → proven real
- Opening: "מה אם הייתי אומר לך שתוך 72 שעות..."
- Works because: Curiosity + dream + "too good to be true" tension
- Best for: Disruptive technology, breakthrough methods

**10. סיפור עם טוויסט (Story with Twist)**
- Starts as failure → flips unexpectedly
- Opening: "בשעה 2:17 בלילה שלחתי הודעה שגרמה לשותף שלי לחשוב שהשתגעתי..."
- Works because: Open loop + drama + specificity (John Carlton style)
- Best for: Products with a strong origin story

**Angle Selection Rules:**
- If you've seen this opening on another sales page — find a different angle
- Surprise > Persuasion. A surprised reader keeps reading; a convinced reader closes the tab
- The angle must FIT the product (death = old thing dying; breakup = leaving a service)

## 9-Section Page Structure

### 1. הוק (Hook / Pre-Headline)
**Purpose:** Break pattern, stop scroll.
- The hook IS the creative angle (obituary, news, confession)
- It's a FRAME, not a summary. Never: "סדנה חד-פעמית עם יהב רובין"
- Must cause "?!מה" before reading the headline

### 2. כותרת ראשית (Headline)
**Purpose:** Present the core benefit so clearly the reader says "exactly what I need!"

**Always write 3 headline options** for the user to choose.

**8 Elements of a winning headline:**
1. Curiosity — must create urge to keep reading
2. Pain — touches the avatar's wound
3. Solution — promises resolution from reader's POV
4. Simple — instantly understood
5. Specific — numbers, descriptions ("72 hours" not "quickly")
6. Proof — why should they believe you?
7. Timeframe — how long until results (certainty for lizard brain)
8. Innovation — why NOW ("in 2026", "the new way")
9. USP (optional) — unique advantage not found elsewhere

**Headline Templates:**

| Template | Formula | Best Performer? |
|----------|---------|-----------------|
| "איך" | "איך לקבל [benefit] תוך [time] אפילו אם [objection]" | Top 3 |
| "תן לי X" | "תן לי [time] ואני אהפוך אותך ל[result]" | Good |
| "הסוד" | "הסוד שיגרום [audience] [result]" | Good |
| **"לפני/אחרי"** | "לקח לי [time] ו[cost] עד ש[result]. ב[product] אני חושף הכל" | **#1 BEST** |
| "שינוי" | "איך הפכתי מ[start] ל[now] תוך [time]" | Excellent |
| "לא חייב להיות" | "אתה לא חייב להיות [X] בשביל [result]" | Good |
| הוק רגשי | The sentence running in the avatar's head | High impact |
| ניפוץ אמונה | Counter-headline with proof | High impact |

### 3. גשר (Bridge)
**Purpose:** Establish authority, create open loop, tease the offer.

**Structure:**
1. Brief authority (numbers, experience)
2. Open Loop — "in 2026 everything changed"
3. Offer teaser — "in 3 days I reveal everything in workshop X"

**Why:** If the offer is innovative enough, give a taste EARLY before diving into pain. Reader stays to read because they know something good is coming.

**Single vs dual presenter:**
- Single presenter: Bridge carries authority + personal eureka story
- Dual presenters: Bridge is shorter; turning point section carries the origin story

### 4. ליד (Lead)
**Purpose:** Create rapport and identification, THEN lead into the sales process.

**IRON RULE: Empathy first, aggression second.** First show you understand them — then they'll let you lead.

**3 Components:**
1. **Visual problem description (מצב מצוי)** — Show the situation they live in, visually. Universal details, not specific times/places.
2. **Give hope (מצב רצוי)** — After identification, promise the solution exists.
3. **Break objections** — "Not [old solution], not [old solution], not [old solution]. This is [new opportunity]."

**11 Lead Types:**

| Type | Opening | Best For |
|------|---------|----------|
| "תדמיין..." | Life after the product | Warm audience, existing authority |
| בעיה | Visual problem description | Pain-aware audience |
| סטורי | Story that creates deep rapport | Products with strong backstory |
| "יש לי סוד" | Secret + story behind it | Raises perceived value |
| רגשי | Pain points as questions → "yes man" | Emotional products |
| "אני לוקח הימור" | "If you entered this page you're probably..." | Creates instant agreement |
| "ההבטחה שלי" | "If you suffer from... by end of page you'll discover..." | Trust building |
| "בין אם את..." | Addresses multiple avatar types | Diverse audience |
| "לא אכפת לי אם..." | Attacks objections upfront | Products with strong objections |
| "אם את מרגישה..." | Problem consequences → "then X is exactly for you" | Daily pain solutions |
| "אם הגעת לפה..." | "Sign that you're... [describes avatar]" | Traffic from ads/stories |

**Choose lead by:** Avatar's awareness level + emotional state entering the page.

### 5. חשיפת הפתרון (Solution Reveal)
**6-part structure:**
1. Shocking statement — "I'm going to say something that will shock..."
2. Kill old method — What I DON'T do anymore
3. Name the solution — Give it a name
4. Simple explanation — How it works in 3 sentences
5. Proof — Screenshots, results, examples
6. Compatibility — NAMED platforms (not "all platforms")

### 6. ההצעה (The Offer)
**Structure:**
1. Product name — big, bold
2. Exclusivity line — "the first and only time..."
3. Format — "3 days, live, hands-on"
4. Detailed breakdown — what happens each day/module (SPECIFIC!)
5. Special element — competition, bonus, personal session

**Breakdown format by product type:**
- Workshop → day-by-day
- Program → phase-by-phase (months)
- Course → module-by-module
- Book → chapter highlights + bonuses

### 7. למי זה (Who It's For)
**Structure:**
1. "Not for everyone" — disqualification builds value
2. "But if you..." — avatar list with ✦
3. Dream — "and you want [specific result]?"
4. Close — "you MUST be inside"

### 8. מחיר + עיגון (Pricing + Anchoring)
**Structure:**
1. Realistic anchor — how much does the old way cost? (ITEMIZED, specific!)
2. "Here you get..." — ✅ list of inclusions
3. Price — big, bold, with "בלבד" (only)
4. Parenthetical — "(includes X. includes Y. full guarantee.)"

### 9. CTA + דחיפות (Urgency)
**Structure:**
1. Real urgency — what happens if not now (competitor advantage, window closing)
2. CTA button — action + what they get ("שריינו לי מקום בסדנה")
3. Below button — scarcity + safety

**CTA text by product type:**
- Application: "רוצה לבדוק אם אני מתאים ✋🏻"
- Direct purchase: "!אני בפנים! שריינו לי מקום"
- Free+shipping: "תשלח לי את הספר"
- NEVER generic "הזמן עכשיו" or "קנה עכשיו"

## Critical Checks (RUN AFTER EVERY SECTION)

### Logical Threading
Every paragraph must connect logically to the next. No jumps.

**Common mistakes:**
- "גם אני הייתי שם" trap: If the presenter is successful (₪100M), they can't say "I was in your shoes" without a specific bridge. Need: "I was great at writing but STILL stuck on building."
- "ידע של 72 שעות" = nonsense. 72 hours is building TIME, not knowledge quantity. Always do a sanity check.

### Specificity = Credibility
- BAD: "הכלים האלה", "פלטפורמות שונות", "תוצאות מרשימות"
- GOOD: "Claude Code, Lovable ו-Base44", "רב מסר, סמוב, שלח מסר", "₪100M+"

### Lead Specificity = Caution
- BAD: "שעה 11 בלילה" — alienates morning workers
- GOOD: Describe the SITUATION, not the time/place
- Universal > Specific when describing the reader's current state

### Pre-headline = Frame, NOT Summary
- BAD: "סדנה חד-פעמית עם יהב רובין" (generic description)
- GOOD: "בצער רב ויגון קודר, אני מודיע על פטירת..." (sets frame)

### Empathy Before Aggression
- BAD: Opening with "אתה מפסיד כסף כל יום!" (pressure)
- GOOD: Opening with "גם אני הייתי תקוע בבנייה. מכיר את התחושה?" (rapport)

### USP + Clarity First
- Reader must understand in 3 seconds what the product does
- Identity shifts must be concrete: "מתלוי במעצב → ל-AI Architect" NOT abstract metaphors
- Enemy must be specific: "המתחרה שכבר אימץ AI" NOT "האלגוריתם"

## Writing Style

| Element | Rule |
|---------|------|
| Sentence length | Short. 3-5 words per line. |
| Line rule | One line = one thought |
| Whispers | Parentheses for intimacy: *(בלי מעצבת. בלי מתכנת.)* |
| Numbers | Non-round. ₪100M+, not "מאות מיליונים". 41,542 ש"ח, not "הרבה כסף". |
| Bold | For key sentences only |
| Questions | Rhetorical for "yes man" effect: "מכיר את התחושה?" |
| Open Loops | "עוד רגע אספר לך מה גיליתי. אבל קודם..." |
| Language | Hebrew, RTL, direct and personal — like a conversation, not an article |
| Emojis | 👈 stats, ✅ results, 💎 bonuses, ✦ bullet lists, 🔥 content items, 👀 attention |
